Vocadeck is an application developed and maintained by an independent developer (deodex-dev), reachable at: vocadeck.support@gmail.com.

3. Technical sub-processors (required third parties)

Vocadeck uses third-party services strictly necessary for the app to function. These providers act as sub-processors and may only use your data for the purposes described here.

  • Google Firebase Authentication — Google Sign-In management. Google LLC
  • Google Cloud Firestore — encrypted synchronization of your cards across devices. Google LLC
  • MyMemory Translated.net — automatic translation (only the word/phrase entered, no identification). Translated SRL

Data sent to MyMemory contains no personal information (only the text to translate).

4. What we do not do

  • ✅ No advertising, targeted or generic
  • ✅ No sale of data to third parties
  • ✅ No sharing of data for commercial purposes
  • ✅ No profiling, no behavioral tracking
  • ✅ No marketing analytics tools (no Google Analytics)

5. Storage and security

Your data is stored locally on your device (SQLite database) and in Google Firestore (Google servers in Europe or the United States). Data in transit is encrypted via TLS. Firebase Firestore security rules ensure no other user can access your cards.

6. Retention period

Your data is retained as long as you use the app. You can delete it at any time via Menu > Delete all data.

7. Your rights (GDPR)

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR / EU 2016/679):

  • Right of access — your data is visible directly in the app.
  • Right of rectification — edit your cards at any time.
  • Right to erasure — "Delete all data" in the menu.
  • Right to portability — your cards are in text format, exportable on request.
  • Right to object — sign out at any time.

To exercise these rights: vocadeck.support@gmail.com

8. Minors

Vocadeck does not target children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data about them.

9. Changes

Any significant changes will be communicated via an app update. The date at the top of this page indicates the current version.
